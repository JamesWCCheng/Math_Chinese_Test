1MC
A car travels from A to B at 40 km/h, from B to C at 60 km/h, and from C back to A at 80 km/h. The distances AB = BC = CA = 120 km. What is the average speed for the entire triangular trip?
Answer: (A) ≈ 54.5 km/h
Time = 120/40 + 120/60 + 120/80 = 3 + 2 + 1.5 = 6.5 h.
Total = 360 km. Average = 360/6.5 ≈ 55.38 km/h → (B).
2MC
A store sells a shirt for $45 after a 25% discount. Later, the same shirt goes on clearance for 60% off the original price. What is the clearance price?
Answer: (B) $24
45 = 0.75P → P = 60. Clearance = 0.40 × 60 = $24.
3MC
Three lines pass through the point (2, k):
• y = 3x − 1
• 2x + y = 9
Which value of k makes this consistent?
Answer: (B) 5
Line 1 at x=2: y = 6−1 = 5. Line 2 at x=2: 4+y=9 → y=5. Both give k = 5. ✓
4MC
The volume V of a cone varies jointly as the square of the radius r and the height h. If V = 150π when r = 5 and h = 18, find V when r = 3 and h = 10.
Answer: (C) 30π
V = kr²h. 150π = k(25)(18) = 450k → k = π/3.
V = (π/3)(9)(10) = 90π/3 = 30π.
5MC
A pharmacist mixes a 15% saline solution with a 45% saline solution to get 600 mL of a 25% solution. How many mL of the 15% solution are needed?
Answer: (C) 400 mL
0.15x + 0.45(600−x) = 0.25(600). 0.15x + 270 − 0.45x = 150.
−0.30x = −120 → x = 400 mL.
6MC
A kayaker paddles 8 km upstream in the same time it takes to paddle 12 km downstream. If the river current is 2 km/h, what is the kayaker's speed in still water?
Answer: (B) 10 km/h
8/(v−2) = 12/(v+2). 8(v+2) = 12(v−2) → 8v+16 = 12v−24 → 4v = 40 → v = 10.

Triangle ABC has vertices A(0, 0), B(10, 0), C(4, 6). What is the area of the triangle?
Answer: 30
Base AB = 10, Height = y-coordinate of C = 6.
Area = ½ × 10 × 6 = 30 square units.
9MC
The braking distance d of a car varies directly as the square of the speed v. At 30 km/h, the braking distance is 9 m. What is the braking distance at 70 km/h?
Answer: (C) 49 m
d = kv². 9 = k(900) → k = 1/100.
d = (70²)/100 = 4900/100 = 49 m.
