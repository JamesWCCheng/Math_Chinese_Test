1MC
A hiker walks uphill at 3 km/h and downhill at 5 km/h on the same path. If the total trip takes 4 hours, what is the one-way distance (uphill)?
Answer: (B) 7.5 km
d/3 + d/5 = 4 → (5d+3d)/15 = 4 → 8d = 60 → d = 7.5 km.
2MC
A rectangle has perimeter 40 cm. Its length is 4 cm more than its width. What is the area of the rectangle?
Answer: (B) 96 cm²
2(l+w) = 40 → l+w = 20. l = w+4 → 2w+4 = 20 → w = 8, l = 12.
Area = 12 × 8 = 96 cm².
3MC
The line 2x + 3y = 18 has x-intercept a and y-intercept b. What is a + b?
Answer: (B) 15
x-int: y=0 → 2x=18 → a=9. y-int: x=0 → 3y=18 → b=6.
a + b = 9 + 6 = 15.
4MC
The gravitational force between two objects varies inversely as the square of the distance and directly as the product of their masses. If F = 20 when m₁ = 4, m₂ = 5, d = 2, find F when m₁ = 6, m₂ = 10, d = 3.
Answer: (B) 26.67
F = km₁m₂/d². 20 = k(4)(5)/4 → 20 = 5k → k = 4.
F = 4(6)(10)/9 = 240/9 ≈ 26.67.
5MC
Three siblings share an inheritance of $45,000 in the ratio 2:3:4. What is the difference between the largest and smallest shares?
Answer: (B) $10,000
Total parts = 9. Smallest = 2/9 × 45000 = 10000. Largest = 4/9 × 45000 = 20000.
Difference = 20000 − 10000 = $10,000.
6MC
A car travels 200 km. For the first 120 km the speed is 60 km/h. What speed must be maintained for the remaining 80 km so the average speed for the whole trip is 50 km/h?
Answer: (B) 40 km/h
Total time needed = 200/50 = 4 h. First part: 120/60 = 2 h.
Remaining time = 2 h for 80 km → speed = 80/2 = 40 km/h.
7MC
The average score of 20 students is 72. The average of the top 8 students is 85. What is the average score of the remaining 12 students?
Answer: (B) 63.3
Total = 20(72) = 1440. Top 8 = 8(85) = 680.
Rest = (1440−680)/12 = 760/12 ≈ 63.3.
8Free Response
A line with slope 2/5 passes through the point (−5, 1). Find the y-coordinate of the point on this line where x = 10.
Answer: 7
y − 1 = (2/5)(x − (−5)) = (2/5)(x+5). At x=10: y−1 = (2/5)(15) = 6 → y = 7.
9MC
A shadow of a 6 ft person is 4 ft long. At the same time, a nearby building casts a 28 ft shadow. How tall is the building?
Answer: (B) 42 ft
6/4 = h/28 → h = 6 × 28/4 = 42 ft. (Direct proportion of similar triangles.)
