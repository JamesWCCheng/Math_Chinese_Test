1MC
A swimmer swims 1.5 km downstream in 30 minutes and the same distance upstream in 50 minutes. What is the swimmer's speed in still water, in km/h?
Answer: (C) 2.7 km/h
Down: 1.5/(0.5) = 3 = v + c. Up: 1.5/(5/6) = 1.8 = v − c.
2v = 4.8 → v = 2.4. Wait — let me recalculate: 50 min = 5/6 h, 1.5/(5/6) = 1.8. So v = (3+1.8)/2 = 2.4. Answer: (B).
2MC
A factory produces Type A widgets ($8 profit each) and Type B widgets ($5 profit each). Last week, 150 widgets yielded a total profit of $960. How many Type A widgets were produced?
Answer: (B) 70
8a + 5(150 − a) = 960 → 8a + 750 − 5a = 960 → 3a = 210 → a = 70.
3MC
Which of the following lines is parallel to 4x − 6y = 9?
Answer: (A)
4x − 6y = 9 → y = ⅔x − 3/2. Slope = ⅔.
Parallel = same slope → y = ⅔x + 1.
4MC
The area of a circle varies directly as the square of its radius. A circle with radius 5 has area 25π. What is the area of a circle with radius 8?
Answer: (C) 64π
A = kr². k = 25π/25 = π. A = π(64) = 64π.
Or simply: (8/5)² × 25π = (64/25)(25π) = 64π.
5MC
An item is listed at $600. It goes through two successive discounts of 20% and 15%. What is the final price?
Answer: (C) $408
After 20% off: 600 × 0.8 = 480. After 15% off: 480 × 0.85 = $408.
Note: Two successive discounts ≠ one 35% discount (which would give $390).
6MC
A 250 m long train passes a person standing on a platform in 10 seconds. How long (in seconds) does the same train take to pass a 150 m long bridge?
Answer: (C) 16 s
Speed = 250/10 = 25 m/s. To pass bridge: distance = 250 + 150 = 400 m.
Time = 400/25 = 16 seconds.
7MC
Company A offers a salary of $30,000 + $2,000 per year of experience. Company B offers $40,000 + $1,500 per year. After how many years of experience do the offers equal?
Answer: (C) 20 years
30000 + 2000n = 40000 + 1500n → 500n = 10000 → n = 20.
8Free Response
A line has x-intercept 6 and y-intercept −4. What is the slope of this line?
Answer: ⅔
Points: (6, 0) and (0, −4). Slope = (0 − (−4))/(6 − 0) = 4/6 = ⅔.
9MC
The resistance R of a wire varies directly as its length L and inversely as the square of its diameter d. If R = 10 ohms when L = 200 m and d = 2 mm, what is R when L = 300 m and d = 3 mm?
Answer: (B) 6.67 ohms
R = kL/d². 10 = k(200)/4 → k = 0.2. R = 0.2(300)/9 = 60/9 ≈ 6.67.
10MC
Faucet A fills a tub in 12 minutes. Faucet B fills it in 18 minutes. The drain empties a full tub in 9 minutes. With both faucets on and the drain open, how long to fill the tub?
Answer: (A) 36 min
Rate = 1/12 + 1/18 − 1/9 = 3/36 + 2/36 − 4/36 = 1/36.
Time = 36 minutes.
