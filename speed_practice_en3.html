🚀 SAT Math: Speed & Rate — Set 4

Advanced Applications — Trains, Wind, and Chase

Time = Distance ÷ Rate
1Multiple Choice
An airplane flies 1,500 miles in 2.5 hours with a tailwind. The return trip against the wind takes 3 hours. What is the wind speed?
Answer: (B) 50 mph
With wind: 1500/2.5 = 600. Against: 1500/3 = 500. s+w = 600, s−w = 500. 2w = 100 → w = 50.
2Multiple Choice
John walks to school at 4 km/h and rides his bike home at 12 km/h. His total travel time is 40 minutes. What is the distance from home to school?
Answer: (B) 2.0 km
d/4 + d/12 = 40/60 = 2/3. (3d+d)/12 = 2/3 → 4d/12 = 2/3 → d = 2 km.
3Multiple Choice
A factory produces 450 units in a 9-hour shift. After upgrading, it produces the same amount in 6 hours. By how many units per hour did production rate increase?
Answer: (C) 25
Old: 450/9 = 50/hr. New: 450/6 = 75/hr. Increase = 25 units/hr.
4Multiple Choice
Car A and Car B start driving toward each other from two cities 396 miles apart. Car A drives at 55 mph and Car B at 77 mph. After how many hours do they meet?
Answer: (B) 3.0 hours
Combined: 55+77 = 132 mph. Time = 396/132 = 3 hours.
5Multiple Choice
A printer prints 30 pages/min. A second prints 45 pages/min. How many seconds will it take both printers together to print 100 pages?
Answer: (C) 80 seconds
Together: 75 pages/min = 1.25 pages/sec. Time = 100/1.25 = 80 seconds.
6Multiple Choice
Emily drives 180 miles. She uses cruise control at 60 mph for part and drives in traffic at 20 mph for the rest. If the total trip takes 5 hours, how many miles were in traffic?
Answer: (C) 60 miles
Let traffic = d. (180−d)/60 + d/20 = 5 → (180+2d)/60 = 5 → 180+2d = 300 → d = 60.
7Multiple Choice
A hiker walks uphill at 2 mph and downhill at 5 mph on the same trail. If the total hike takes 7 hours, how long is the trail one way?
Answer: (B) 10 miles
d/2 + d/5 = 7. 7d/10 = 7 → d = 10 miles.
8Multiple Choice
A leaky faucet drips at 2 cups per hour. There are 16 cups in a gallon. How many gallons of water are wasted in one week?
Answer: (B) 21 gallons
2 cups/hr × 24 hr × 7 days = 336 cups. 336/16 = 21 gallons.
9Multiple Choice
A 200-meter train overtakes a 300-meter train. The faster train goes 72 km/h and the slower 54 km/h. How many seconds to completely pass?
Answer: (C) 100 seconds
Relative speed = 18 km/h = 5 m/s. Distance = 200+300 = 500 m. Time = 500/5 = 100 s.
10Free ResponseSAT Grid-In
A courier delivers a package 15 miles away in 30 minutes. Maintaining the same speed, how many miles can the driver cover in 2 hours?
Answer: 60 miles
Speed = 15/0.5 = 30 mph. In 2 hours: 30 × 2 = 60 miles.
