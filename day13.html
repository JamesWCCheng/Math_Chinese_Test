1MC
Two trains leave stations 500 km apart at the same time, heading toward each other. Train A goes 80 km/h, Train B goes 120 km/h. After how many hours do they meet?
Answer: (B) 2.5 hours
Combined speed = 80 + 120 = 200 km/h. Time = 500/200 = 2.5 h.
2MC
Store A sells 3 kg of apples for $12. Store B sells 5 kg for $18. Which store offers the better price per kg, and by how much?
Answer: (B) Store B, by $0.40/kg
A: $12/3 = $4/kg. B: $18/5 = $3.60/kg.
B is cheaper by 4 − 3.6 = $0.40/kg.
3MC
What is the equation of the line through (2, 5) and (6, 13) in the form y = mx + b?
Answer: (A) y = 2x + 1
m = (13−5)/(6−2) = 8/4 = 2. b = 5 − 2(2) = 1. y = 2x + 1.
4MC
The illumination from a lamp varies inversely as the square of the distance. At 3 m, it is 80 lux. At what distance is it 20 lux?
Answer: (B) 6 m
I = k/d². 80 = k/9 → k = 720. 20 = 720/d² → d² = 36 → d = 6 m.
5MC
Five test scores have a mean of 76. Adding a sixth score changes the mean to 78. What is the sixth score?
Answer: (C) 88
Sum of 5 = 380. New sum of 6 = 468. Sixth score = 468 − 380 = 88.
6MC
A boat takes 2 hours to go 24 km upstream and 1.5 hours to return downstream. What is the boat's speed in still water?
Answer: (B) 14 km/h
Up: 24/2 = 12 = v − c. Down: 24/1.5 = 16 = v + c.
2v = 28 → v = 14 km/h.
7MC
A family spends 30% of income on rent, 20% on food, 15% on transportation, and saves the rest. If they save $700/month, what is the monthly income?
Answer: (B) $2,000
Savings = 100% − 30% − 20% − 15% = 35%. 0.35 × Income = 700 → Income = $2,000.
8Free Response
Line L₁: y = ½x + 3 and Line L₂: y = −2x + 8. Find the coordinates of their intersection.
Answer: (2, 4)
½x + 3 = −2x + 8 → ½x + 2x = 5 → 5x/2 = 5 → x = 2. y = ½(2)+3 = 4.
Point: (2, 4).
9MC
A model car is built at a scale of 1:24. If the model is 18 cm long, how long is the real car in meters?
Answer: (B) 4.32 m
Real = 18 × 24 = 432 cm = 4.32 m.
10MC
Worker A makes 40 parts/hour, B makes 60 parts/hour. After A works alone for 2 hours, both work together. How many total hours from start to complete 400 parts?
Answer: (B) 5.2 h
A alone 2 h: 80 parts. Remaining: 320. Together: 100/hr → 320/100 = 3.2 h.
Total = 2 + 3.2 = 5.2 hours.
