1MC
A person walking on a moving escalator takes 20 steps in 10 seconds. The escalator has 40 visible steps. How many seconds would it take to ride the escalator standing still?
Answer: (B) 20 s
Walking speed = 20 steps / 10 s = 2 steps/s. In 10 s, escalator moved 40−20 = 20 steps.
Escalator speed = 2 steps/s. Standing still: 40/2 = 20 seconds.
2MC
On a math test, correct answers earn 5 points and wrong answers lose 2 points. A student answered all 30 questions and scored 94 points. How many correct answers?
Answer: (B) 22
5c − 2(30−c) = 94 → 5c − 60 + 2c = 94 → 7c = 154 → c = 22.
3MC
A temperature of 68°F equals 20°C. A temperature of 212°F equals 100°C. If F and C have a linear relationship, what Celsius temperature equals 95°F?
Answer: (B) 35°C
Slope = (100−20)/(212−68) = 80/144 = 5/9.
C − 20 = (5/9)(F − 68). At F=95: C = 20 + (5/9)(27) = 20 + 15 = 35°C.
4MC
A spring stretches 6 cm under a 15 N force (Hooke's Law: F = kx). What force is needed to stretch it 10 cm?
Answer: (B) 25 N
F = kx. 15 = 6k → k = 2.5 N/cm. F = 2.5 × 10 = 25 N.
5MC
A company's revenue is $8 per unit and cost is $3 per unit plus $2000 fixed cost. How many units must be sold to break even?
Answer: (B) 400
Revenue = Cost: 8n = 3n + 2000 → 5n = 2000 → n = 400 units.

7MC
A phone plan charges $30/month + $0.10/text. Another charges $45/month with unlimited texting. How many texts make the plans equal?
Answer: (B) 150
30 + 0.10t = 45 → 0.10t = 15 → t = 150 texts.
8Free Response
A line is parallel to 5x − 3y = 15 and has y-intercept 7. What is the x-intercept of this line?
Answer: −21/5 = −4.2
Slope of 5x−3y=15: m = 5/3. Parallel line: y = (5/3)x + 7.
x-intercept: 0 = (5/3)x + 7 → x = −21/5 = −4.2.
9MC
The time to fill a swimming pool varies inversely as the number of hoses. 4 hoses fill it in 6 hours. How long with 3 hoses?
Answer: (B) 8 h
k = 4 × 6 = 24. Time with 3 hoses = 24/3 = 8 hours.
10MC
A tank is ¼ full. After adding 30 liters, it becomes ⅝ full. What is the capacity of the tank?
Answer: (C) 80 L
30 liters = ⅝ − ¼ = 5/8 − 2/8 = 3/8 of capacity.
Capacity = 30 ÷ (3/8) = 30 × 8/3 = 80 L.
