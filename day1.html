1MC
A delivery driver drives 60 km at 40 km/h, then 90 km at 60 km/h, and finally 30 km at 30 km/h. What is the average speed for the entire trip, in km/h?
Answer: (C) 45 km/h
Time = 60/40 + 90/60 + 30/30 = 1.5 + 1.5 + 1 = 4 hours.
Total distance = 60 + 90 + 30 = 180 km.
Average speed = 180 ÷ 4 = 45 km/h.
⚠️ Do NOT average the three speeds: (40+60+30)/3 = 43⅓ is wrong!
2MC
A theater sold 400 tickets. Adult tickets cost $12 each and child tickets cost $7 each. If the total revenue was $3,800, how many adult tickets were sold?
Answer: (C) 200
Let a = adult, c = child. a + c = 400, 12a + 7c = 3800.
Substituting c = 400 − a: 12a + 7(400 − a) = 3800 → 5a = 1000 → a = 200.
3MC
Line L is perpendicular to 2x + 5y = 10 and passes through the point (4, −1). What is the y-intercept of line L?
Answer: (B) −11
Slope of 2x + 5y = 10 is −2/5. Perpendicular slope = 5/2.
y − (−1) = 5/2 (x − 4) → y = 5x/2 − 10 − 1 = 5x/2 − 11.
y-intercept = −11.
4MC
The number of days needed to complete a construction project varies inversely with the number of workers. If 8 workers can finish the project in 15 days, how many additional workers are needed to finish it in 10 days?
Answer: (B) 4
Inverse variation: workers × days = constant. k = 8 × 15 = 120.
Workers needed = 120 ÷ 10 = 12.
Additional = 12 − 8 = 4 workers.
5MC
A chemist has 400 mL of a 35% acid solution. How many mL of pure acid must be added to make the concentration exactly 50%?
Answer: (C) 120 mL
Current acid = 0.35 × 400 = 140 mL. Let x = pure acid added.
(140 + x) / (400 + x) = 0.50 → 140 + x = 200 + 0.5x → 0.5x = 60 → x = 120.
6MC
Alex leaves Town A heading toward Town B at 50 km/h. One hour later, Ben leaves Town B heading toward Town A at 70 km/h. The two towns are 290 km apart. How many hours after Ben departs do they meet?
Answer: (B) 2 hours
In Alex's 1-hour head start, he covers 50 km. Remaining gap = 290 − 50 = 240 km.
Combined closing rate = 50 + 70 = 120 km/h.
Time = 240 ÷ 120 = 2 hours after Ben departs.
7MC
For what value of a does the system of equations
3x − 2y = 5
ax + 4y = 7
have no solution?
Answer: (A) −6
No solution means parallel lines: same slope, different intercept.
Slope of line 1: 3/2. Slope of line 2: −a/4.
3/2 = −a/4 → a = −6.
Check intercepts differ: 5/2 ≠ 7/4 ✓, so no solution confirmed.
8Free Response
The line 3x + 4y = 24 forms a triangle with the x-axis and y-axis. What is the area of this triangle?
Answer: 24
x-intercept: set y = 0 → x = 8. y-intercept: set x = 0 → y = 6.
Area = ½ × 8 × 6 = 24 square units.
9MC
The variable z varies directly as x² and inversely as y. When x = 3 and y = 6, z = 12. What is z when x = 4 and y = 8?
Answer: (C) 16
z = kx²/y. Using (3, 6, 12): 12 = k·9/6 → k = 8.
z = 8 × 16 / 8 = 16.
10MC
Pipe A fills a tank in 6 hours, Pipe B fills it in 8 hours, and Pipe C drains it in 12 hours. If all three pipes are opened simultaneously, how many hours will it take to fill the tank?
Answer: (C) 4.8 hours
Net rate = 1/6 + 1/8 − 1/12 = 4/24 + 3/24 − 2/24 = 5/24 per hour.
Time = 24/5 = 4.8 hours.
