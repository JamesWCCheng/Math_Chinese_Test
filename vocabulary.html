Equations & Algebra (方程式與代數)

linear equation
一元一次方程式
An equation of the first degree containing a single variable (e.g., ax + b = c).
system of equations
二元一次聯立方程式
A set of two or more equations sharing variables that are solved simultaneously. Also called simultaneous equations.
variable
變數
A symbol (usually a letter like x, y) representing an unknown numerical value.
coefficient
係數
A numerical multiplier multiplied by a variable (e.g., in 5x, 5 is the coefficient).
constant
常數 / 常數項
A fixed value or number in an expression that does not change.
substitution method
代入消去法
Solving a system by expressing one variable in terms of another and substituting it.
elimination method
加減消去法
Solving a system by adding or subtracting equations to eliminate a variable.
distributive property
分配律
Multiplying a single term by two or more terms inside parentheses: a(b + c) = ab + ac.

Inequalities (不等式)

inequality
不等式
A mathematical statement comparing values using symbols like >, <, ≥, or ≤.
compound inequality
聯立不等式 / 雙邊不等式
Two inequalities joined together, such as -2 ≤ x < 5 or x > 3 and x < 8.
consecutive integers
連續整數
Integers that follow each other in order without gaps (e.g., n, n+1, n+2).
consecutive odd integers
連續奇數
Odd integers following each other (e.g., 3, 5, 7 or n, n+2, n+4 where n is odd).
solution set
解的集合 / 解集
The complete range or set of all values that satisfy a given inequality.
satisfied
滿足 / 使成立
When a value substituted into an equation or inequality makes the statement true.

Coordinate Geometry & Lines (解析幾何與直線)

perpendicular
垂直
Intersecting at a right angle (90°). Perpendicular slopes are negative reciprocals: m×m' = -1.
parallel
平行
Lines in the same plane that never intersect. Parallel lines have equal slopes: m₁ = m₂.
slope (gradient)
斜率
A measure of the steepness of a line, defined as vertical change / horizontal change (rise over run).
y-intercept
y軸截距 / y截距
The point where a line crosses the y-axis (where x = 0).
x-intercept
x軸截距 / x截距
The point where a line crosses the x-axis (where y = 0).
slope-intercept form
斜截式
The equation of a straight line in the form y = mx + b, where m is the slope and b is the y-intercept.
point-slope form
點斜式
The equation of a line in the form y - y₁ = m(x - x₁), using a point and the slope.
standard form of a line
直線一般式
The equation of a straight line written as Ax + By = C, where A, B, and C are integers.
collinear
共線
Points that lie on the same straight line.
midpoint
中點
The center point of a line segment, calculated as ((x₁+x₂)/2, (y₁+y₂)/2).

Proportion & Variation (比例與變分)

ratio
比 / 比率
A comparison of two quantities, often written as a fraction or with a colon (e.g., a:b).
proportion
比例 / 比例式
An equation stating that two ratios are equal (e.g., a/b = c/d).
direct variation
正比
A relationship where one variable is a constant multiple of another (y = kx). Also stated as "directly proportional".
inverse variation
反比
A relationship where the product of two variables is constant (xy = k or y = k/x). Also stated as "inversely proportional".
joint variation
聯合變分
A variation where one variable varies directly as the product of two or more other variables (y = kxz).
combined variation
複合變分
A variation containing both direct variation and inverse variation relationships (e.g., y = kx/z).
constant of variation
變分常數
The non-zero constant k in direct, inverse, or joint variation equations.
gear ratio
齒輪比
The ratio of the number of teeth or rotation speeds between two meshing gears (inversely proportional).

Word Problems & Finance (應用與商業名詞)

average speed
平均速率
Total distance traveled divided by the total time taken (Avg = Total d / Total t).
upstream / downstream
逆水 / 順水
Moving against the current (speed = boat - current) or with the current (speed = boat + current).
headwind / tailwind
逆風 / 順風
Moving against the wind (speed = plane - wind) or with the wind (speed = plane + wind).
work rate
工作效率
The amount of work done per unit of time (Rate = Work / Time).
mixture
混合物 / 混合溶液
A combination of substances (e.g. mixing saline or acid solutions of different concentrations).
dilution
稀釋
Reducing the concentration of a solution by adding pure water/solvent.
marked price (list price)
定價 / 標價
The original price set on an item before discount.
selling price
售價
The actual price at which an item is sold after discount or markup.
discount
折扣
A reduction in price. (e.g., 20% discount is equivalent to paying 80% of the price).
markup
利潤加成 / 漲價
The amount added to the cost price of goods to cover overhead and profit (e.g. markup of 20% on cost).
break-even point
損益兩平點
The production/sales level where total cost equals total revenue, resulting in zero net profit/loss.
simple interest
單利
Interest calculated only on the initial principal amount invested (Interest = Principal × Rate × Time).
