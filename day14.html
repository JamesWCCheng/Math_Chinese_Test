3MC
A line has equation 2y − 6x = 10. What is its slope and y-intercept?
Answer: (A) slope = 3, y-intercept = 5
2y = 6x + 10 → y = 3x + 5. Slope = 3, y-int = 5.
4MC
A population of bacteria doubles every 3 hours. If the initial count is 500, what is the population after 9 hours?
Answer: (B) 4,000
9 hours = 3 doubling periods. 500 × 2³ = 500 × 8 = 4,000.
5MC

Answer: (B) 75 g
Gold fraction = 3/10. Gold = (3/10) × 250 = 75 g.
6MC
A car uses 6 liters of fuel per 100 km. If fuel costs $1.50/liter, how much does a 450 km trip cost?
Answer: (B) $40.50
Fuel = 6 × 4.5 = 27 liters. Cost = 27 × 1.50 = $40.50.
7MC
If 5 machines produce 200 items in 4 hours, how many items can 8 machines produce in 6 hours?
Answer: (B) 480
Rate per machine per hour = 200/(5×4) = 10 items.
8 machines × 6 hours × 10 = 480 items.
8Free Response
The line y = kx − 3 is perpendicular to the line through (1, 4) and (3, 10). What is k?
Answer: k = −⅓
Slope of line through (1,4) and (3,10) = (10−4)/(3−1) = 3.
Perpendicular slope = −1/3 → k = −⅓.
9MC
The load a beam can support varies directly as the width and the square of the depth, and inversely as the length. A beam 2 m wide, 3 m deep, and 4 m long supports 900 kg. What does a beam 3 m wide, 2 m deep, and 6 m long support?
Answer: (B) 400 kg
L = kwd²/l. 900 = k(2)(9)/4 = 18k/4 → k = 200.
L = 200(3)(4)/6 = 2400/6 = 400 kg.
