1MC
A boat travels 48 km upstream in 4 hours and returns downstream in 3 hours. What is the speed of the current, in km/h?
Answer: (B) 2 km/h
Upstream speed = 48/4 = 12 = v − c. Downstream speed = 48/3 = 16 = v + c.
Subtracting: 2c = 4 → c = 2. (Boat speed v = 14 km/h.)
2MC
A jar contains nickels (5¢) and dimes (10¢). There are 50 coins worth a total of $3.85. How many dimes are in the jar?
Answer: (C) 27
n + d = 50 and 5n + 10d = 385. Substitute n = 50 − d:
5(50 − d) + 10d = 385 → 250 + 5d = 385 → d = 27.
3MC
Points A(1, 3), B(4, k), and C(7, 11) are collinear. What is the value of k?
Answer: (C) 7
Collinear → equal slopes. Slope AC = (11 − 3)/(7 − 1) = 8/6 = 4/3.
Slope AB = (k − 3)/3 = 4/3 → k − 3 = 4 → k = 7.
4MC
z varies jointly as x and y. When x = 3 and y = 5, z = 45. What is z when x = 4 and y = 7?
Answer: (C) 84
z = kxy. 45 = k(3)(5) = 15k → k = 3.
z = 3(4)(7) = 84.
5MC
In a club, the ratio of boys to girls is 5 : 3. After 4 boys leave and 8 girls join, the ratio becomes 1 : 1. How many members were in the club originally?
Answer: (C) 48
Boys = 5k, Girls = 3k. After change: 5k − 4 = 3k + 8 → 2k = 12 → k = 6.
Original total = 5(6) + 3(6) = 48.
6MC
A train 200 m long passes a 300 m platform in 25 seconds. What is the train's speed, in m/s?
Answer: (C) 20 m/s
The train must cover its own length plus the platform: 200 + 300 = 500 m.
Speed = 500 / 25 = 20 m/s.
7MC
For what value of k does the system kx + 3y = 9 and 2x + y = 3 have infinitely many solutions?
Answer: (D) 6
Infinitely many solutions: the equations represent the same line.
k/2 = 3/1 = 9/3 → all ratios must equal 3. k/2 = 3 → k = 6.
8Free Response
A plumber charges a $50 service fee plus $35 per hour. An electrician charges an $80 service fee plus $25 per hour. After how many hours will their total charges be equal?
Answer: 3 hours
Plumber: 50 + 35h. Electrician: 80 + 25h.
50 + 35h = 80 + 25h → 10h = 30 → h = 3.
9MC
Two gears are meshed together. Gear A has 24 teeth and Gear B has 36 teeth. If Gear A rotates at 90 RPM, how fast does Gear B rotate?
Answer: (C) 60 RPM
Teeth × RPM is constant for meshed gears: 24 × 90 = 36 × x → x = 60 RPM.
(Inverse variation: more teeth = slower rotation.)
10MC
A store marks up an item by 60% above cost, then offers a 25% discount during a sale. If the final selling price is $480, what was the original cost?
Answer: (C) $400
Marked price = 1.6C. Sale price = 0.75 × 1.6C = 1.2C.
1.2C = 480 → C = $400.
