3MC
The line y = −2x + 8 crosses the x-axis at A and the y-axis at B. What is the area of triangle OAB (O is the origin)?
Answer: (C) 16
A = (4, 0), B = (0, 8). Area = ½ × 4 × 8 = 16.

5MC
A shop owner buys 100 items at $5 each. He sells 60 at $8 each and the rest at $4 each. What is his overall profit percentage?
Answer: (B) 28%
Cost = 500. Revenue = 60(8) + 40(4) = 480 + 160 = 640.
Profit = 140. Profit% = 140/500 = 28%.
6MC
A motorboat can travel 36 km upstream in the same time it takes to travel 48 km downstream. If the current speed is 2 km/h, what is the boat's speed in still water?
Answer: (C) 14
36/(v−2) = 48/(v+2). 36(v+2) = 48(v−2) → 36v+72 = 48v−96 → 12v = 168 → v = 14 km/h.
7MC
The sum of two numbers is 100. Three times the larger minus twice the smaller equals 120. What is the larger number?
Answer: (C) 64
x + y = 100, 3x − 2y = 120. From first: y = 100 − x.
3x − 2(100−x) = 120 → 5x = 320 → x = 64.
8Free Response
A water tank has capacity 600 liters. Currently it is ⅓ full. Water flows in at 20 L/min and drains out at 5 L/min. How many minutes to fill the tank?
Answer: 26⅔ minutes
Current water = 200 L. Need 400 more. Net rate = 20 − 5 = 15 L/min.
Time = 400/15 = 26⅔ min ≈ 26.67 min.
9MC
The intensity of light varies inversely as the square of the distance from the source. At 2 m, the intensity is 100 units. At what distance is the intensity 25 units?
Answer: (B) 4 m
I = k/d². 100 = k/4 → k = 400. 25 = 400/d² → d² = 16 → d = 4 m.
10MC
The slope of line L is 3. L passes through the point where x + y = 7 and 2x − y = 8 intersect. What is the equation of L?
Answer: (A) y = 3x − 13
Intersection: x + y = 7, 2x − y = 8 → 3x = 15 → x = 5, y = 2. Point (5, 2).
y − 2 = 3(x − 5) → y = 3x − 15 + 2 = 3x − 13.
