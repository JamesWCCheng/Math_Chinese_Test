⚖️ SAT Math: Variation — Set 3

Mixed & Advanced Proportion Problems

Joint: z = kxy  |  Combined: z = kx/y
1MC
If 8 identical machines produce 240 parts in 3 hours, how many parts can 5 machines produce in 6 hours?
Answer: (C) 300
Rate per machine per hour = 240/(8×3) = 10. 5 machines × 6 hrs × 10 = 300.
2MC
The weight of a metal rod varies directly with its length. A 15 cm rod weighs 120 grams. How much does a 25 cm rod weigh?
Answer: (B) 200 g
k = 120/15 = 8 g/cm. Weight = 8×25 = 200 g.
3MC
The intensity of light varies inversely as the square of the distance from the source. At 2 m, intensity is 100 units. What is the intensity at 5 m?
Answer: (B) 16
I = k/d². k = 100×4 = 400. I = 400/25 = 16.
4MC
A and B share $600 in the ratio 3:5. How much does B receive?
Answer: (D) $375
B's share = 600 × 5/8 = $375.
5MC
z varies jointly as x and y. If z = 60 when x = 3 and y = 4, what is z when x = 5 and y = 2?
Answer: (B) 50
z = kxy. 60 = k(3)(4) → k = 5. z = 5(5)(2) = 50.
6MC
A scale model of a building is built at 1:200 ratio. If the model is 15 cm tall, how tall is the actual building in meters?
Answer: (B) 30 m
Actual = 15 × 200 = 3000 cm = 30 m.
7MC
If 6 painters paint a house in 4 days, and 2 painters get sick on day 1 (after 0 work done), how many days will the remaining painters need?
Answer: (B) 6
k = 6×4 = 24 painter-days. With 4 painters: 24/4 = 6 days.
8MC
The ratio of boys to girls in a class is 4:5. If there are 36 students total, how many boys are there?
Answer: (B) 16
Total parts = 9. Boys = 36 × 4/9 = 16.
9Free ResponseGrid-In
y varies directly as x and inversely as z. When x = 6 and z = 2, y = 15. What is y when x = 10 and z = 3?
Answer: y = 50/3 ≈ 16.67
y = kx/z. 15 = k(6)/2 → k = 5. y = 5(10)/3 = 50/3.
10MC
A recipe for 4 people uses 2 cups of rice. For a party of 14 people, how many cups of rice are needed?
Answer: (C) 7
2/4 = x/14 → x = 2×14/4 = 7 cups.
