🚀 SAT Math: Speed & Rate — Set 2

10 More Questions to Master d = r × t

Distance = Rate × Time

💡 Remember

Average Speed = Total Distance ÷ Total Time — never average two speeds directly!
1Multiple Choice
A jogger runs 5 miles in 40 minutes. What is her average speed in miles per hour?
Answer: (C) 7.5 mph
40 min = 2/3 hour. Speed = 5 ÷ (2/3) = 5 × 3/2 = 7.5 mph.
2Multiple Choice
A bus travels at 45 mph for 2 hours, then at 60 mph for 1.5 hours. What is the total distance traveled?
Answer: (C) 180 miles
45×2 + 60×1.5 = 90 + 90 = 180 miles.
3Multiple Choice
If it takes 3 hours to drive from Town A to Town B at 60 mph, how long would it take at 45 mph?
Answer: (B) 4 hours
Distance = 60 × 3 = 180 miles. At 45 mph: 180 ÷ 45 = 4 hours.
4Multiple Choice
A boat travels 24 miles upstream at 8 mph and returns downstream at 12 mph. What is the average speed for the round trip?
Answer: (B) 9.6 mph
Upstream: 24/8 = 3 hrs. Downstream: 24/12 = 2 hrs. Total: 48 mi in 5 hrs = 9.6 mph.
⚠️ NOT (8+12)/2 = 10!
5Multiple Choice
Two runners start at the same point on a circular track. Runner A completes a lap in 6 minutes, Runner B in 8 minutes. After how many minutes will they first meet again at the starting point?
Answer: (C) 24 minutes
LCM(6, 8) = 24. Runner A completes 4 laps, Runner B completes 3 laps.
6Multiple Choice
A person drives 150 miles. The first 90 miles are on the highway at 45 mph, and the remaining distance is on local roads at 30 mph. What is the total travel time?
Answer: (C) 4 hours
Highway: 90/45 = 2 hrs. Local: 60/30 = 2 hrs. Total = 4 hours.
7Multiple Choice
A pipe fills a tank in 6 hours. A second pipe fills it in 4 hours. If both pipes are open, how long does it take to fill the tank?
Answer: (B) 2.4 hours
Combined rate = 1/6 + 1/4 = 5/12 per hour. Time = 12/5 = 2.4 hours.
8Multiple Choice
A train 150 meters long passes a platform 250 meters long in 20 seconds. What is the speed of the train in meters per second?
Answer: (C) 20 m/s
Total distance = 150 + 250 = 400 m. Speed = 400 ÷ 20 = 20 m/s.
9Free ResponseSAT Grid-In
Alex can mow a lawn in 3 hours. Ben can mow the same lawn in 5 hours. If they work together for 1 hour, what fraction of the lawn is left unmowed?
Answer: 7/15
Combined rate = 1/3 + 1/5 = 8/15 per hour. After 1 hour: 8/15 done. Left = 7/15.
10Multiple Choice
A car gets 30 miles per gallon. If gas costs $4.00 per gallon, how much does it cost in fuel to drive 360 miles?
Answer: (C) $48
Gallons needed = 360 ÷ 30 = 12. Cost = 12 × $4 = $48.
