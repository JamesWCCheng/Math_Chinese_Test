1MC
A car drives from City A to City B at 60 km/h and returns by the same route at 40 km/h. What is the average speed for the entire round trip?
Answer: (A) 48 km/h
For equal-distance legs, use the harmonic mean: 2 × 60 × 40 / (60 + 40) = 4800 / 100 = 48.
⚠️ The arithmetic mean (60+40)/2 = 50 is a classic trap!
2MC
Sara invested a total of $10,000 in two accounts. One earns 4% annual interest, the other earns 7%. After one year, her total interest was $520. How much did she invest at the 7% rate?
Answer: (B) $4,000
Let x = amount at 4%. 0.04x + 0.07(10000 − x) = 520.
700 − 0.03x = 520 → x = 6000 at 4%.
Amount at 7% = 10000 − 6000 = $4,000.
3MC
The line y = mx + 2 passes through the intersection of x + 2y = 8 and 3x − y = 3. What is the value of m?
Answer: (C) ½
Solve the system: 3x − y = 3 → y = 3x − 3. Substitute into x + 2(3x − 3) = 8 → 7x = 14 → x = 2, y = 3.
Point (2, 3) on y = mx + 2: 3 = 2m + 2 → m = ½.
4MC
The force between two magnetic poles varies inversely as the square of the distance between them. When the distance is 3 cm, the force is 36 N. What is the force when the distance is 6 cm?
Answer: (B) 9 N
F = k/d². 36 = k/9 → k = 324.
F = 324/36 = 9 N.
Shortcut: distance doubled → force = 36/4 = 9 (inverse square).
5MC
A tank contains 200 liters of a 40% salt solution. How many liters of pure water must be added to reduce the concentration to 25%?
Answer: (C) 120 liters
Salt stays constant at 0.4 × 200 = 80 L. Adding w liters of water:
80 / (200 + w) = 0.25 → 200 + w = 320 → w = 120.
6MC
Two cyclists start at the same point on a 400 m circular track, going in the same direction. One cycles at 8 m/s, the other at 6 m/s. After how many seconds do they first meet again at the same point?
Answer: (C) 200 seconds
Relative speed = 8 − 6 = 2 m/s. The faster cyclist must lap the slower by exactly one track length.
Time = 400 / 2 = 200 seconds.
7MC
Tom is 4 years older than Jerry. Five years ago, Tom was exactly twice Jerry's age. How old is Tom now?
Answer: (C) 13
Let Jerry's age = J. Tom = J + 4.
Five years ago: (J + 4 − 5) = 2(J − 5) → J − 1 = 2J − 10 → J = 9.
Tom = 9 + 4 = 13.
8MC
A line parallel to y = −3x + 5 passes through the point (2, 7). What is the y-intercept of this line?
Answer: (D) 13
Parallel → same slope = −3. Using point (2, 7):
y − 7 = −3(x − 2) → y = −3x + 6 + 7 → y = −3x + 13.
9MC
The cost of a circular pizza varies directly as the square of its diameter. A 10-inch pizza costs $8. How much should a 15-inch pizza cost?
Answer: (C) $18
C = kd². 8 = k(100) → k = 0.08.
C = 0.08 × 225 = $18.
Alternatively: (15/10)² × 8 = 2.25 × 8 = 18.
10MC
Alice can paint a room in 5 hours, Bob in 8 hours. They work together for 2 hours, then Alice leaves. How many more hours does Bob need to finish alone?
Answer: (B) 2.8 hours
Combined rate = 1/5 + 1/8 = 13/40 per hour. In 2 hours: 26/40 = 13/20 done.
Remaining = 7/20. Bob alone at 1/8 per hour: (7/20) ÷ (1/8) = 56/20 = 2.8 hours.
