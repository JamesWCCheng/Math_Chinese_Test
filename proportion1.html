⚖️ SAT Math: Variation — Set 1

Direct & Inverse Proportion

Direct: y = kx  |  Inverse: xy = k

💡 Key Concepts

Direct variation: y/x is constant (y = kx).
Inverse variation: x×y is constant (y = k/x).
To identify: if one doubles and the other doubles → direct. If one doubles and the other halves → inverse.
1MC
If y varies directly as x, and y = 12 when x = 4, what is y when x = 10?
Answer: (B) 30
k = 12/4 = 3. y = 3×10 = 30.
2MC
If y varies inversely as x, and y = 6 when x = 8, what is y when x = 12?
Answer: (B) 4
k = 6×8 = 48. y = 48/12 = 4.
3MC
The cost of gasoline is directly proportional to the number of gallons purchased. If 5 gallons cost $20, how much do 13 gallons cost?
Answer: (C) $52
Rate = $20/5 = $4/gal. 13 × $4 = $52.
4MC
12 workers can complete a job in 10 days. If the job must be finished in 6 days, how many workers are needed? (Workers and days are inversely proportional.)
Answer: (C) 20
k = 12×10 = 120. Workers = 120/6 = 20.
5MC
Which table represents a direct variation?
(A) x: 2,4,6 → y: 5,10,15
(B) x: 1,2,3 → y: 12,6,4
(C) x: 2,3,5 → y: 8,11,17
(D) x: 1,4,9 → y: 3,5,7
Answer: (A)
y/x = 5/2 = 10/4 = 15/6 = 2.5 (constant ratio) → direct variation.
6MC
The volume of gas in a container is inversely proportional to its pressure. If the volume is 300 mL at 2 atm, what is the volume at 5 atm?
Answer: (B) 120
k = 300×2 = 600. V = 600/5 = 120 mL.
7MC
If y varies directly as x², and y = 50 when x = 5, what is y when x = 3?
Answer: (B) 18
y = kx². 50 = k(25) → k = 2. y = 2(9) = 18.
8MC
A recipe uses 3 cups of flour for 24 cookies. How many cups are needed for 40 cookies?
Answer: (C) 5
Direct proportion: 3/24 = x/40 → x = 3×40/24 = 5 cups.

The time to fill a pool is inversely proportional to the flow rate. At 8 liters/min it takes 90 minutes. How many minutes at 12 liters/min?
Answer: 60 minutes
k = 8×90 = 720. t = 720/12 = 60.
10MC
If y is directly proportional to x, and the graph passes through (4, 10), what is the constant of proportionality k?
Answer: (B) 2.5
y = kx → 10 = 4k → k = 2.5.
