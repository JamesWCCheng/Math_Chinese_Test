1MC
Solve for x:
0.25(x - 4) + x/2 = 5
Answer: (B) 8
Rewrite 0.25 as a fraction: ¼(x - 4) + x/2 = 5.
Multiply both sides by 4: (x - 4) + 2x = 20.
Combine terms: 3x - 4 = 20.
Add 4: 3x = 24.
Divide by 3: x = 8.
2MC
Solve the system of equations for x:
0.75x - 0.25y = 2.25
0.5x + y = 5
Answer: (C) 4
Multiply Equation 1 by 4: 3x - y = 9.
Multiply Equation 2 by 2: x + 2y = 10.
From the first simplified equation, y = 3x - 9.
Substitute y into the second equation: x + 2(3x - 9) = 10 → x + 6x - 18 = 10 → 7x = 28 → x = 4.
(Substituting x = 4 back gives y = 3).
3MC
Solve the inequality for x:
2(x - 1)/3 - 3(x + 1)/5 > 1/15
Answer: (C) x > 20
Multiply all terms by 15 to clear denominators:
10(x - 1) - 9(x + 1) > 1.
Expand: 10x - 10 - 9x - 9 > 1.
Combine terms: x - 19 > 1.
Add 19 to both sides: x > 20.
4MC
A manufacturing factory has fixed operating costs of $2,000 per day and variable costs of $15 per unit produced. If each unit is sold for $25, how many units must the factory produce and sell per day to break even?
Answer: (C) 200
Let n = number of units produced and sold.
Total Cost = 2,000 + 15n.
Total Revenue = 25n.
To break even, Revenue must equal Cost: 25n = 2,000 + 15n.
Subtract 15n: 10n = 2,000.
Divide by 10: n = 200 units.
5Free Response
A researcher wants to mix 4 liters of a 15% acid solution with a 30% acid solution to make a final mixture that is exactly 20% acid. How many liters of the 30% solution should be added?
Answer: 2
Let x = liters of the 30% solution to add.
Acid in the 15% solution = 4 liters × 0.15 = 0.60 liters.
Acid in the 30% solution = x liters × 0.30 = 0.30x liters.
Total final mixture volume = 4 + x liters.
Set up concentration equation: (0.60 + 0.30x) / (4 + x) = 0.20.
Multiply by (4 + x): 0.60 + 0.30x = 0.20(4 + x) → 0.60 + 0.30x = 0.80 + 0.20x.
Subtract 0.20x: 0.60 + 0.10x = 0.80.
Subtract 0.60: 0.10x = 0.20 → x = 2 liters.
