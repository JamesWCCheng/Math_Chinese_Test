1MC
A 300 m train overtakes a 200 m train. The faster train travels at 72 km/h and the slower at 54 km/h. How many seconds does it take for the faster train to completely pass the slower one?
Answer: (B) 100 s
Relative speed = 72 − 54 = 18 km/h = 5 m/s.
Distance = 300 + 200 = 500 m. Time = 500/5 = 100 s.
2MC
The present ages of a father and son are in the ratio 7 : 2. In 10 years, the ratio will be 9 : 4. What is the father's present age?
Answer: (B) 35
Father = 7k, Son = 2k. In 10 years: (7k+10)/(2k+10) = 9/4.
28k + 40 = 18k + 90 → 10k = 50 → k = 5. Father = 35.
3MC
Convert 3x − 4y + 12 = 0 to slope-intercept form and identify the y-intercept.
Answer: (A) 3
3x − 4y + 12 = 0 → 4y = 3x + 12 → y = ¾x + 3.

w varies directly as x and inversely as y². When x = 8, y = 2, w = 14. Find w when x = 5, y = 5.
Answer: (C) 2.8
w = kx/y². 14 = 8k/4 → k = 7. w = 7(5)/25 = 35/25 = 1.4.
Correction: k = 14×4/8 = 7. w = 7×5/25 = 1.4. Answer: (A).
5MC
500 mL of 30% sugar solution is mixed with 300 mL of 50% sugar solution. What is the resulting concentration?
Answer: (B) 37.5%
Sugar = 0.3(500) + 0.5(300) = 150 + 150 = 300 mL.
Total = 800 mL. Concentration = 300/800 = 37.5%.
6MC
A runner completes the first half of a race at 10 km/h and the second half at 6 km/h. If the total distance is 24 km, what is the average speed for the entire race?
Answer: (B) 7.5 km/h
Each half = 12 km. Time₁ = 12/10 = 1.2 h. Time₂ = 12/6 = 2 h.
Total = 24 km in 3.2 h. Avg = 24/3.2 = 7.5 km/h.

8Free Response
The line connecting (−2, 5) and (4, −1) has a midpoint M. What are the coordinates of M?
Answer: (1, 2)
M = ((−2+4)/2, (5+(−1))/2) = (2/2, 4/2) = (1, 2).
9MC
The frequency of a vibrating string varies inversely as its length. A 60 cm string vibrates at 440 Hz. What frequency does a 40 cm string produce?
Answer: (C) 660 Hz
fL = k. 440 × 60 = 26400. f = 26400/40 = 660 Hz.
