1MC
A bus travels 120 km at a certain speed. If it had gone 20 km/h faster, the trip would have taken 1 hour less. What was the original speed?
Answer: (B) 40 km/h
120/v − 120/(v+20) = 1. Try v=40: 3 − 2 = 1 ✓. Original speed = 40 km/h.
2MC
A farmer has chickens and cows. Together they have 50 heads and 140 legs. How many cows are there?
Answer: (B) 20
c + k = 50, 2c + 4k = 140. From first: c = 50 − k. 2(50−k) + 4k = 140 → 100 + 2k = 140 → k = 20 cows.
3MC
What is the distance between the points (−3, 4) and (5, −2)?
Answer: (B) 10
d = √[(5−(−3))² + (−2−4)²] = √[64 + 36] = √100 = 10.
4MC
If y varies directly as the cube of x, and y = 54 when x = 3, what is y when x = 2?
Answer: (B) 16
y = kx³. 54 = 27k → k = 2. y = 2(8) = 16.
5MC
A sum of $12,000 is divided among A, B, and C in the ratio 3 : 4 : 5. How much does B receive?
Answer: (B) $4,000
Total parts = 12. B's share = 4/12 × 12000 = $4,000.
6MC
Two cars travel from City X to City Y. Car P travels at 80 km/h and Car Q at 100 km/h. Car Q leaves 45 minutes after Car P. They arrive at the same time. What is the distance from X to Y?
Answer: (C) 300 km
P travels d/80 hours, Q travels d/100 hours. d/80 − d/100 = 0.75.
(5d − 4d)/400 = 0.75 → d = 300 km.
7MC
For the system 2x + 3y = 12 and 4x + 6y = k, for what value of k does the system have no solution?
Answer: (C) 20
The second equation is 2× the first when k = 24 (infinite solutions).
For no solution: same slope but different intercept, so k ≠ 24. Any k ≠ 24 gives no solution since 2/4 = 3/6 = 1/2 but 12/k ≠ 1/2. k = 20 satisfies this (12/20 ≠ 1/2).
8Free Response
A line passes through (1, 4) and (5, 12). Write its equation in slope-intercept form (y = mx + b). What is the value of b?
Answer: b = 2
m = (12−4)/(5−1) = 8/4 = 2. y − 4 = 2(x − 1) → y = 2x + 2.
9MC
The weight an elevator cable can support varies directly as the square of its diameter. A 2 cm cable supports 800 kg. What weight can a 3 cm cable support?
Answer: (C) 1800 kg
W = kd². 800 = 4k → k = 200. W = 200(9) = 1800 kg.
10MC
Tank A fills a pool in 8 hours, Tank B in 12 hours. They run together for 3 hours, then both stop. What fraction of the pool is still empty?
Answer: (A) 3/8
Rate = 1/8 + 1/12 = 5/24. In 3 hours: 15/24 = 5/8 done.
Empty = 1 − 5/8 = 3/8.
