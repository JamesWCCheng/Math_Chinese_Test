1MC
Two runners start at the same point on a 400 m circular track, running in opposite directions. Runner A runs at 5 m/s and Runner B at 3 m/s. How many seconds until they meet for the second time?
Answer: (B) 100 s
Combined closing speed = 5 + 3 = 8 m/s. First meet at 400/8 = 50 s.
Second meet at 2 × 50 = 100 s.
2MC
A store blends premium coffee ($15/kg) with standard coffee ($9/kg) to make 20 kg of a blend costing $11.40/kg total. How many kg of premium coffee are used?
Answer: (B) 8 kg
15p + 9(20 − p) = 228. 15p + 180 − 9p = 228 → 6p = 48 → p = 8.
3MC
The midpoint of segment PQ is M(3, 5). If P = (−1, 2), what is the slope of line PQ?
Answer: (B) ¾
Q = (2·3−(−1), 2·5−2) = (7, 8).
Slope = (8−2)/(7−(−1)) = 6/8 = ¾.
4MC
When the length of a pendulum is quadrupled, its period doubles. Which type of variation does the period follow with respect to pendulum length?
Answer: (C) Direct as square root
T = k√L. If L → 4L, then T = k√(4L) = 2k√L = 2T. ✓
Period varies directly as the square root of the length.
5MC
A merchant buys an item for $200 and marks it up by 80%. During a sale, a 30% discount is offered. What is the profit?
Answer: (C) $52 profit
Marked = 200 × 1.8 = $360. Sale = 360 × 0.7 = $252.
Profit = 252 − 200 = $52.
6MC
A truck averaging 60 km/h takes 30 minutes longer than a car averaging 80 km/h to travel the same distance. What is the distance?
Answer: (C) 120 km
d/60 − d/80 = 0.5. Finding LCD: (4d − 3d)/240 = 0.5 → d = 120 km.
7MC
A two-digit number has digits that sum to 11. Reversing the digits gives a number that is 27 less than the original. What is the original number?
Answer: (D) 74
a + b = 11. (10a + b) − (10b + a) = 27 → 9(a − b) = 27 → a − b = 3.
a = 7, b = 4 → number = 74.
8MC
What is the area of the triangle formed by the line y = 2x, the x-axis, and the vertical line x = 5?
Answer: (C) 25
Vertices: (0,0), (5,0), (5,10). Base = 5, height = 10.
Area = ½ × 5 × 10 = 25.
9MC
The volume of a gas varies inversely with its pressure at constant temperature. If the volume is 500 mL at 2 atm, what is the volume at 5 atm?
Answer: (B) 200 mL
PV = k. 2 × 500 = 1000. V = 1000/5 = 200 mL.
10MC
Alex can complete a job in 12 days, Ben in 18 days. They work together for 4 days, then Ben quits. How many more days does Alex need to finish alone?
Answer: (C) 5⅓ days
Combined rate = 1/12 + 1/18 = 5/36. In 4 days: 20/36 = 5/9 done.
Remaining: 4/9. Alex alone: (4/9)÷(1/12) = 48/9 = 16/3 ≈ 5⅓ days.
