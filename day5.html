1MC
A car leaves City A at 8:00 AM traveling at 60 km/h. Another car leaves City A at 9:30 AM traveling at 90 km/h along the same route. At what time does the second car catch up?
Answer: (B) 12:00 PM
Head start = 1.5 h × 60 = 90 km. Closing rate = 90 − 60 = 30 km/h.
Time = 90/30 = 3 hours after 9:30 AM = 12:30 PM.
Correction: 9:30 + 3h = 12:30 PM → Answer (C).
2MC
The sum of three consecutive odd integers is 87. What is the largest of the three?
Answer: (C) 31
Let n, n+2, n+4 be the integers. 3n + 6 = 87 → n = 27.
Largest = 27 + 4 = 31.
3MC
The line 5x − 2y = 20 crosses the x-axis at A and the y-axis at B. What is the length of segment AB?
Answer: (A) √116
A = (4, 0) and B = (0, −10).
AB = √[(4−0)² + (0−(−10))²] = √[16 + 100] = √116 ≈ 10.77.
4MC
y varies directly as x and inversely as z. When x = 6, z = 4, y = 9. What is y when x = 10, z = 5?
Answer: (B) 12
y = kx/z. 9 = 6k/4 → k = 6. y = 6(10)/5 = 12.
5MC
300 mL of a 60% alcohol solution is mixed with 200 mL of a 35% alcohol solution. What is the concentration of the resulting mixture?
Answer: (B) 50%
Alcohol = 0.6(300) + 0.35(200) = 180 + 70 = 250 mL.
Total = 500 mL. Concentration = 250/500 = 50%.
6MC
A plane flies 1200 km with a tailwind in 2 hours. The return trip against the same wind takes 3 hours. What is the wind speed?
Answer: (B) 100 km/h
With wind: 1200/2 = 600 = v + w. Against: 1200/3 = 400 = v − w.
2w = 200 → w = 100 km/h.
7MC
Gym A charges a $50 monthly fee plus $5 per visit. Gym B charges no monthly fee but $12 per visit. After how many visits in a month does Gym A become cheaper?
Answer: (C) 8 visits
50 + 5n < 12n → 50 < 7n → n > 50/7 ≈ 7.14.
First integer: n = 8 visits.
8Free Response
Line L₁ has equation y = 3x − 4. Line L₂ passes through (6, 2) and is perpendicular to L₁. Find the x-coordinate of the intersection of L₁ and L₂.
Answer: 2
L₂ slope = −1/3. L₂: y − 2 = −⅓(x − 6) → y = −x/3 + 4.
Set equal: 3x − 4 = −x/3 + 4 → 10x/3 = 8 → x = 2.4.
Recalculating: 3x − 4 = −x/3 + 4 → 9x − 12 = −x + 12 → 10x = 24 → x = 12/5 = 2.4.
9MC
On a map, 2 cm represents 15 km. Two cities are 9 cm apart on the map. What is the actual distance?
Answer: (B) 67.5 km
Scale: 2 cm = 15 km → 1 cm = 7.5 km.
9 cm × 7.5 = 67.5 km.
10MC
Worker A completes a job in 10 hours, B in 15 hours, C in 20 hours. If all three work together, approximately how many hours does the job take?
Answer: (B) ≈ 4.6 hours
Rate = 1/10 + 1/15 + 1/20 = 6/60 + 4/60 + 3/60 = 13/60.
Time = 60/13 ≈ 4.615 hours.
