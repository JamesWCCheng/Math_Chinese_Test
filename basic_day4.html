1MC
Solve for x:
2x - 3(x - 4) = 7x - 20
Answer: (C) 4
Expand parentheses: 2x - 3x + 12 = 7x - 20.
Simplify: -x + 12 = 7x - 20.
Add x to both sides: 12 = 8x - 20.
Add 20 to both sides: 32 = 8x.
Divide by 8: x = 4.
2MC
Solve the system of equations for y:
½x + y = 5
2x - y = 5
Answer: (C) 3
Express y from the second equation: y = 2x - 5.
Substitute y into the first equation: ½x + (2x - 5) = 5.
Combine terms: 2.5x - 5 = 5 → 2.5x = 10 → x = 4.
Find y: y = 2(4) - 5 = 8 - 5 = 3.
3MC
Solve the system of inequalities for x:
2x - 1 ≥ 5
3x - 4 < 14
Answer: (A) 3 ≤ x < 6
Solve inequality 1: 2x ≥ 6 → x ≥ 3.
Solve inequality 2: 3x < 18 → x < 6.
Combine both ranges: 3 ≤ x < 6.
4MC
A bicyclist rides from Town A to Town B at an average speed of 12 miles per hour. She returns along the same route at an average speed of 8 miles per hour. If the round trip takes a total of 5 hours, what is the distance between Town A and Town B in miles?
Answer: (C) 24
Let d = distance between towns.
Time to go = d / 12.
Time to return = d / 8.
Total time: d / 12 + d / 8 = 5.
Multiply by 24 to clear denominators: 2d + 3d = 120 → 5d = 120 → d = 24 miles.
5Free Response
Pipe A can fill a water tank in 4 hours, while Pipe B can fill the same tank in 6 hours. If both pipes are opened at the same time, how many hours will it take to fill the tank? (Write as a decimal)
Answer: 2.4
Pipe A's rate = 1/4 of the tank per hour.
Pipe B's rate = 1/6 of the tank per hour.
Combined rate = 1/4 + 1/6 = 3/12 + 2/12 = 5/12 of the tank per hour.
Time to fill = 1 / (5/12) = 12/5 = 2.4 hours.
