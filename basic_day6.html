1MC
Solve for x:
0.3(x - 2) - 0.15 = 1.2
Answer: (C) 6.5
Multiply all terms by 100 to eliminate decimals:
30(x - 2) - 15 = 120.
Expand: 30x - 60 - 15 = 120 → 30x - 75 = 120.
Add 75 to both sides: 30x = 195.
Divide by 30: x = 6.5.
2MC
Solve the system of equations for x:
4x + 5y = 7
3x - 2y = 11
Answer: (C) 3
Multiply the first equation by 2: 8x + 10y = 14.
Multiply the second equation by 5: 15x - 10y = 55.
Add both modified equations: (8x + 15x) = 14 + 55 → 23x = 69.
Divide by 23: x = 3.
(Substituting x = 3 into the first equation yields y = -1).
3MC
Solve the compound inequality for x:
-5 < 2x - 3 ≤ 7
Answer: (A) -1 < x ≤ 5
Add 3 to all three parts: -5 + 3 < 2x ≤ 7 + 3 → -2 < 2x ≤ 10.
Divide all parts by 2: -1 < x ≤ 5.
4MC
A vehicle travels at an average speed of 45 mph for the first 2 hours of a trip, and then travels at 60 mph for the next 3 hours. What is the average speed of the vehicle for the entire 5-hour trip in mph?
Answer: (C) 54
Distance for segment 1 = 45 mph × 2 hours = 90 miles.
Distance for segment 2 = 60 mph × 3 hours = 180 miles.
Total distance = 90 + 180 = 270 miles.
Total time = 2 + 3 = 5 hours.
Average speed = 270 miles / 5 hours = 54 mph.
5Free Response
A chemist needs to dilute 300 mL of a 40% acid solution to make it a 30% acid solution. How many mL of pure water must be added?
Answer: 100
Amount of pure acid in the original solution = 300 mL × 0.40 = 120 mL.
Let w = volume of water to add.
The new total volume will be 300 + w.
To have a 30% concentration: 120 / (300 + w) = 0.30.
Multiply out: 120 = 0.30(300 + w) → 120 = 90 + 0.30w.
Solve: 30 = 0.30w → w = 30 / 0.30 = 100 mL.
