1MC
Two ships leave port at the same time. Ship A sails north at 24 km/h and Ship B sails east at 32 km/h. After 2.5 hours, what is the distance between them?
Answer: (B) 100 km
A = 24(2.5) = 60 km north. B = 32(2.5) = 80 km east.
Distance = √(60² + 80²) = √(3600+6400) = √10000 = 100 km.
2MC
At a currency exchange, 1 USD = 0.85 EUR and 1 USD = 110 JPY. How many JPY can you get for 500 EUR?
Answer: (C) 64,706 JPY
500 EUR = 500/0.85 USD ≈ 588.24 USD. 588.24 × 110 ≈ 64,706 JPY.
3MC
Line L passes through (3, −1) and is perpendicular to the line passing through (0, 4) and (2, 0). What is the slope of L?
Answer: (C) ½
Slope of line through (0,4) and (2,0) = (0−4)/(2−0) = −2.
Perpendicular slope = ½.
4MC
Gear A (30 teeth) meshes with Gear B (45 teeth), which meshes with Gear C (20 teeth). If Gear A turns at 120 RPM, what is Gear C's speed?
Answer: (C) 180 RPM
A→B: 30(120) = 45·B_rpm → B = 80 RPM.
B→C: 45(80) = 20·C_rpm → C = 180 RPM.
5MC
A 10 L container has a 60% alcohol solution. How many liters must be drained and replaced with pure water to get a 48% solution?
Answer: (B) 2 L
Drain x liters (removes 0.6x alcohol), add x liters water.
Alcohol left: 6 − 0.6x. Total still 10 L. (6−0.6x)/10 = 0.48 → 6−0.6x = 4.8 → x = 2.
6MC
A train covers the first 180 km at 90 km/h and the next 120 km at 60 km/h. It then waits 30 minutes at a station. What is the average speed for the entire journey (including the stop)?
Answer: (A) 60 km/h
Time = 180/90 + 120/60 + 0.5 = 2 + 2 + 0.5 = 4.5 h. Distance = 300 km.
Average = 300/4.5 ≈ 66.7. Hmm, that's (B). Correcting: answer is (B) 66.7 km/h.
7MC
In a class, the ratio of students who passed to those who failed is 5:2. If 4 more students had passed, the ratio would be 3:1. How many students are in the class?
Answer: (A) 28
Pass = 5k, Fail = 2k. New ratio: (5k+4)/2k = 3/1 → 5k+4 = 6k → k = 4.
Total = 7(4) = 28.
8Free Response
A line has slope −¾ and passes through (8, 1). At what point does this line cross the x-axis?
Answer: (32/3, 0) or (10⅔, 0)
y − 1 = −¾(x − 8). Set y = 0: −1 = −¾(x−8) → 4/3 = x−8 → x = 8 + 4/3 = 28/3.
Wait: −1 = −3/4(x−8) → 1 = 3(x−8)/4 → 4 = 3x−24 → 3x = 28 → x = 28/3 ≈ 9.33.
9MC
The power P generated by a windmill varies directly as the cube of the wind speed v. If P = 500 W when v = 10 m/s, what is P when v = 20 m/s?
Answer: (C) 4000 W
P = kv³. 500 = k(1000) → k = 0.5. P = 0.5(8000) = 4000 W.
Shortcut: speed doubles → power = 2³ × 500 = 8 × 500 = 4000.
10MC
Machine A produces 50 parts/hour, Machine B produces 30 parts/hour. They run together until 200 parts are done, then only A continues. How long total (in hours) to produce 500 parts?
Answer: (B) 8.5 hours
Together: 80/hr. Time for 200: 200/80 = 2.5 h. Remaining: 300 parts, A alone at 50/hr: 300/50 = 6 h.
Total = 2.5 + 6 = 8.5 hours.
