1MC
Solve for x:
3(2x - 5) = 2(x + 2) - 3
Answer: (C) 4
Expand both sides: 6x - 15 = 2x + 4 - 3 → 6x - 15 = 2x + 1.
Subtract 2x from both sides: 4x - 15 = 1.
Add 15 to both sides: 4x = 16.
Divide by 4: x = 4.
2MC
Solve the system of equations for x:
y = 3x - 7
2x + 3y = 23
Answer: (C) 4
Substitute y into the second equation: 2x + 3(3x - 7) = 23.
Expand: 2x + 9x - 21 = 23 → 11x - 21 = 23.
Add 21: 11x = 44.
Divide by 11: x = 4.
(Substituting x = 4 back gives y = 5).
3MC
Solve the inequality for x:
⅓(2 - x) ≥ -1
Answer: (B) x ≤ 5
Multiply both sides by 3: 2 - x ≥ -3.
Subtract 2 from both sides: -x ≥ -5.
Divide by -1 and reverse the inequality sign: x ≤ 5.
4MC
In a school, the ratio of boys to girls is 4:5. If there are 120 more girls than boys, what is the total number of students in the school?
Answer: (C) 1080
Let number of boys = 4k, girls = 5k.
Given difference: 5k - 4k = 120 → k = 120.
Total students = 4k + 5k = 9k = 9 × 120 = 1,080 students.
5Free Response
A swimming pool is being filled by two pipes. Pipe A can fill the pool in 8 hours, and Pipe B can fill it in 12 hours. If both pipes are opened together, but Pipe B is turned off after 3 hours, how many total hours will it take to fill the pool?
Answer: 6
Let t = total hours to fill the pool. Pipe A runs for t hours, and Pipe B runs for 3 hours.
Set up the work equation: t / 8 + 3 / 12 = 1.
Simplify: t / 8 + 1 / 4 = 1.
Subtract 1/4: t / 8 = 3 / 4.
Solve: t = 8 × (3/4) = 6 hours.
